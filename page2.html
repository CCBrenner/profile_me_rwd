Family

Photo Gallery

I grew up more with my om's side of the family and less with my dad's family due to them living far away. My grandfather on my mother's side, who I take after the most, is the only living grandparent I still have alive. I see my extended family a lot less now since I decided to go to school out-of-state which would bring my family ultimately to follow me to the Nashville area.


I have quite a few relatives who are quite scientifically-minded. One of my grandfathers was a chemist, the other was a sewing machine manager, my dad is an information technology project manager, my aunt and uncle are both physician assistants, I have a cousin who is a bio-chemist, her sister is going to school to be a nurse, and I have another uncle who is in the computer science field and his son is going into graphic design. It is cool to see the variety in occupation within such a close group of people.


All-in-all I have a lot of family. My mom has a brother and a sister and my dad has one sister and four brothers. Almost all have kids. On my dad's side of the family because he is so young my cousins are the age that a typical uncle or aunt would be and I am the same age as my second counsins.


Family is the first line of defense and support. It really shows when the tough days come that family matters more than anything in the world. I am glad to have so much in common with them all, I wouldn't wish for any other family than mine.
